Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries regarding revenue services and county permits.

What is the deadline for paying annual Land Rates?

Annual land rates are due on or before March 31st of every calendar year. Outstanding land rates after this date attract penalty interest at 1% per month compounded, in accordance with the Meru County Rating Act.

How is the Single Business Permit (SBP) fee determined?

The permit fee is calculated based on: (1) The type of business activity (Retail, Transport, Hospitality, Agriculture, etc.), (2) The size of the business (number of staff, floor area), and (3) The location zone (Urban, Peri-Urban, or Rural). You can estimate your SBP cost using the Fee Calculator on this website.

Is cash accepted at Mt Kenya House offices?

No, the Meru County Government is 100% cashless. Cash payments at bank counters or county offices are not accepted. All payments should be made using M-Pesa Paybill 440112, direct bank deposits to designated county accounts, or online through our self-service portal.

Our Services

County Revenue Services

Select a category below to view all available services and their fees from the Meru County Finance Act 2019

🏢 Business & Trade

Single Business Permits (SBP), Trade Licenses, and Commercial fees for all businesses operating within Meru County. Includes retail shops, wholesale traders, kiosks, and hawkers.

🚗 Transport & Parking

Daily and monthly parking fees for all vehicle types including motorbikes, cars, matatus, buses, and trucks. Also covers impounding fees, towing charges, and vehicle registration.

🌍 Land & Property

Land rates, property taxes, plot fees, building approvals, and land clearance certificates. Includes commercial and residential property rates as per the Valuation Roll.

🏥 Health & Licensing

Food hygiene licenses, health inspections, medical facility registration, vaccination certificates, and public health permits for businesses and institutions.

🌾 Agriculture & Cess

Agricultural produce cess for crops like miraa, tea, coffee, maize, and vegetables. Also covers livestock transport fees, slaughterhouse licenses, and meat inspection charges.

🏗️ Construction & Building

Building plan approvals, construction permits, structural inspections, occupation certificates, and fire safety compliance fees for residential and commercial buildings.

🍺 Alcohol Licenses

Liquor licenses for bars, restaurants, night clubs, hotels, and distributors. Includes application fees, annual license fees, and renewal charges as per the Alcoholic Drinks Control Act.

🗑️ Waste Management

Refuse collection charges for residential and commercial premises, garbage disposal fees, environmental compliance, and penalties for illegal dumping.

Overview

About Meru County Revenue Board

The Meru County Revenue Board (MCRB) is the official revenue administration body of the County Government of Meru, established under the Meru County Revenue Board Act, 2014. Our mandate is to oversee, assess, collect, and account for all charges, levies, rates, and fees imposed by the County Government of Meru in a fair, efficient, and transparent manner.

Our Vision

To be a model of excellence in automated local revenue administration, facilitating a prosperous and self-sustaining Meru County.

Our Mission

To maximize revenue collection through customer-centric service, innovative technology, transparent administration, and absolute integrity.
